ABOUT AGIPMOTEL MBARARA

Agip Motel is a 3-star hotel in Mbarara, Western Region, Uganda with free breakfast and restaurant A restaurant, a bar/lounge, and 24-hour room service are available at this hotel. Free continental breakfast, free WiFi in public areas and free self-parking are also provided. Additionally, a business center, a meeting room, and concierge services are onsite.

All 18 rooms offer free WiFi, 24-hour room service, and TVs with satellite channels. Ceiling fans, showers, and desks are among the other amenities that guests will find. Agip Motel offers 18 accommodations with complimentary toiletries and ceiling fans. Satellite television is provided. Bathrooms include showers. Guests can surf the web using complimentary wireless Internet access. Housekeeping is provided daily.

A guest’s satisfaction is our priority. Agip Motel is a favorite hotel for tourists visiting the western national parks due to its location, services and accommodation.

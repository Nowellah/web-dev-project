Agip Motel Mbarara

Agip Motel, with its excellent location, diverse facilities, quality service and professionalism is the number one stop for anyone going through or stopping in Mbarara. Its friendly and fast service provided by a highly trained staff and its well-kept and pleasant rooms makes Agip Motel the preferred place for international travelers, holiday makers, local businessmen, women and families. Guests can enjoy the cool morning or evening breeze as they watch the birds in our small bamboo forest. You can sit, relax and enjoy the spectacular surroundings as you listen to nature’s bird songs. For the more adventurous bird-watching groups, this stopover is ideal for viewing the Ugandan species. A guest’s satisfaction is our priority.
